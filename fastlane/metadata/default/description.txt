Uniks is a privacy-first personal tracker that turns everyday notes into structured insights.

Type naturally — "Ran 5km in 28min" — and Uniks extracts category, value, unit, tags, and notes using on-device AI. No cloud required. No account. No telemetry.

FEATURES
• Global QuickInput HUD on macOS (Cmd + Shift + U)
• Natural-language capture on iOS
• Local AI parsing with MLX, Ollama, or deterministic mock engine
• Searchable event history
• Dashboard with category totals, trends, top tags, and daily activity
• Manual editing and retry when AI misses the mark
• Export your data anytime

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
Everything is stored locally on your device. The AI runs locally too, so your personal logs never leave your Mac or iPhone.

Perfect for quantified-self enthusiasts, habit builders, and anyone who wants to track their life without giving their data away.
